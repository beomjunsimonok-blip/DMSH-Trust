Our Mission

DMSH Trust exists to solve a problem traditional estate planning ignores. Modern life lives inside devices: smartphones, laptops, encrypted drives, cloud backups, private communications, and confidential digital archives. We provide a legally documented execution system for physical digital devices and their contents. We do not interpret. We do not improvise. We execute instructions exactly as written.

Our History

While financial assets transfer through trusts and probate, personal digital devices remain legally ambiguous. Executors frequently face locked phones, encrypted drives, unknown passwords, and sensitive data without guidance. DMSH Trust was created to provide a structured, instruction-based solution for these situations.

How It Works

1. Define Instructions

Members document specific instructions for each device and data category.

2. Secure Archive

Instructions are cryptographically archived and legally validated.

3. Activation Event

Execution begins only after verified incapacitation or death confirmation.

4. Controlled Execution

Devices are collected, processed, and instructions executed exactly as documented.
