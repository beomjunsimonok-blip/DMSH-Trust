FAQ

What do you actually handle?

Photos, files, browser history and the device according to the client's instructions.

No. We never access social media, email, or online accounts.

How is the device sent?

The designated contact ships the device to DMSH Trust once the trigger event is confirmed.

Is the process audited?

Yes. Internal audit procedures and documented chain-of-custody logs ensure proper handling.
