Your devices may outlive you — and so will everything inside them.

Phones, laptops, and storage devices often contain private history, sensitive records, and personal material that families are unprepared to handle.

What families face

Private photos mixed with sensitive material
Search history and personal files left behind
Important memories next to information that should never be exposed
Family members forced to guess what should be deleted or preserved

What goes wrong without a plan

Valuable memories are lost
Private material is accidentally exposed
Devices passed on without review
No one knows what the trustor intended
