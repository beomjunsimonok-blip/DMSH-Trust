Our Mission

To provide a discreet, instruction-based digital asset trust service focused exclusively on physical digital devices and the confidential data within them. We execute your directives exactly as written — nothing more, nothing less.

Our History

Traditional estate planning addresses financial portfolios but rarely addresses smartphones, laptops, private files, browsing history, or sensitive digital information. DMSH Trust was established to bridge that gap.

How It Works

1. You define legally binding digital instructions.

2. Upon verified incapacitation, devices are securely collected via insured mail.

3. We delete, transfer, preserve, or liquidate assets exactly as directed, with full documentation provided to beneficiaries.
